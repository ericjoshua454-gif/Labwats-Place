Jollof Rice +

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 6 large tomatoes (blended)
  • 4 red bell peppers (blended)
  • 2 onions
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tsp curry powder, thyme, and seasoning cubes
  • Salt and vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and 1 onion into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil, fry sliced onion and tomato paste for 3 mins.
  3. Add blended mixture, curry, thyme, and seasoning. Cook until sauce thickens.
  4. Add rice and chicken stock, stir, then cover with foil for steaming.
  5. Cook on low heat until rice is soft and smoky. Serve with chicken or plantain.

Cooking Time: 1 hr | Serves: 6

Fried Rice +

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups parboiled rice

  • 1 cup diced liver or chicken
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • Salt, seasoning cubes, and oil

Instructions:

  1. Boil rice with chicken stock until 70% cooked, then set aside.
  2. Saut√© onions, liver, and vegetables in oil with curry and seasoning.
  3. Add rice in batches to stir-fry, mixing evenly with vegetables.
  4. Cook on low heat for 10 more minutes. Serve hot with grilled chicken.

Cooking Time: 45 mins | Serves: 5

Egusi Soup +

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups ground melon seeds (egusi)
  • 1 cup palm oil
  • 500g assorted meat (goat, beef, tripe)
  • 2 cups spinach or bitterleaf
  • 2 tbsp ground crayfish
  • 2 scotch bonnets
  • Seasoning cubes and salt

Instructions:

  1. Boil meat with seasoning until tender. Keep stock aside.
  2. Heat palm oil, fry onions and ground egusi paste until crumbly.
  3. Add meat stock, crayfish, pepper, and meat. Simmer 15 mins.
  4. Add leafy vegetables, stir well, and cook 5 mins more.
  5. Serve hot with pounded yam, fufu, or eba.

Cooking Time: 1 hr | Serves: 6
